Vearin Terril

Human Male: Age: 31 Earth cycles
Current Location: Mining Crawler GIC-1187362

Bio:

Vearin Terril grew up under the name of Kyriakos Mareth, in a city which was once called New York. When he was young he would code robotics, and was liked to tinker with AI coding. This changed however when he was 13, and a rival nation attacked the City. During this attack, most of his family was killed by Tenebrous City's own AI defense network. Since then Vearin has had a vindeta against AI's.

During the rebuilding of New York (renamed Tenebrous City due to the twisted nature of most of its buildings), it became apperent that the government only cared for using the attack as propeganda to build fortifications, and expand the military. The people of the city were left to fend for themselves, and Vearin started plotting to takeover Tenebrous City, to try and improve living conditions for the masses. Years later he joined the AMSR (Advanced Military Space Reserves) for training, and access to their advanced technologie in preperation for this takeover.

Leaving the AMSR on good terms, he iniciated the takeover. The attack was successful, and with relativly little bloodshed. Too successful in fact. The government agreed to his terms, but he was branded a Terrorist, and was forced to leave Earth and flee to JSC space. Once on one of the moons of Jupiter he changed his name to Vearin Terril, and used the skills he learned with the AMSR to start a Defense Contracting Business.

His DCB has become very successful, expanding to the point of being able to rival small governments. What started out as a small business of one, has become large enough that even high profile corporations such as GIC, and IIC, contract out to him. The UNE still occasionally sends bounty hunters after Vearin, but these have slowed down once the Hunters realized just how dangerous he was. It became a point where the Bounty Hunters guild has put a minimum Bounty out for Vearin because he is so dangerous.

A point to be noted is that, he is so well liked by those he employs that they gladly intercept, and eliminate the bounty hunters before they reach him. Vearin for his part was extremly annoyed when he found out they did this. He does not want them to risk themselves for 'his' battles.

Embedding a Google map using iFrame:

Embedding a Google Map or Route into a webpage is very similar to embedding a normal iFrame. You just need the source, and optional CSS styling. There is a Google Maps API you can use, but you do not have to.

To obtain a Google Map iFrame you can, go to maps.google.com and search for a place or business. Once you have your desired location you will need to get the iFrame code. To do so, look on the left side of the screen, where you typed your search query, is the directions/navigation panel, on the right side of this panel is a "share" button, click that and select the "Embed a Map" tab. Readjust your map view as desired, and then copy the code it generated. Past this iFrame code into your webpage HTML, and you are done.

This is just the basic iFrame, you can now style it to fit into the rest of page. To do so you can wrap the iFrame in a div or section that has an ID, or Class, and use these to modify the iFrame's size, and minor style adjustments. The Google GUI itself however cannot be modified.
